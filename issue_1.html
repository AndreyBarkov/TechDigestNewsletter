Articles

 

 

 

New to Jamstack? Everything You Need to Know to Get Started

by Colby Fayock 

 

 

 

 

 

A significant number of static websites on the web prefer to run on JAMstack. It stands for Javascript, API, Markup. This relatively new approach to build and deliver websites offers better resiliency, lower maintenance costs, faster loading times, and a more straightforward development cycle. This article is a great entry point for JamStack.

 

 

 

 

 

 

Iterables & Iterators - An In-Depth JavaScript Tutorial

by Mateusz Podlasin

 

 

 

 

 

If you don't know the difference between iterables and iterators, you should definitively check out this article.

It goes fast from a basic explanation of those concepts down to taking apart the whole thing piece by piece and writing your own iterables and iterators in JavaScript.

 


 

 

 

 

 

How I cut GTA Online loading times by 70%

by T0ST

 

 

 

 

 

Grand Theft Auto Online is a widely popular online game with insane loading launch time by today's standards. A real-life example of how seemingly simple computing tasks can become a huge bottleneck when the scale increases. This article is a bit of a technical detective story with simple morale in the end. I hope you'll enjoy it.
